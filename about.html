About Us

Renowned firm of Tamil Nadu, Saravana Stores group started its jewelry showroom in 2003 in the name of Saravana Stores Elite Pvt Ltd at Chennai. Later in 2006, this showroom was extended to provide more space, comfort, and convenience to its customers and enhance their shopping experience. SST is the first-ever showroom in India with a separate floor for each item. Uncompromising quality and customer satisfaction are always at the forefront of SST. What\'s more the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) has rated us as \'Hallmark\' which is awarded by the Govt. of India in the year 2003. Our strict emphasis on quality is combined with the latest technology to offer our customers a competitive edge. SST offers a wide range of Gold, Diamond, Platinum & Silver ornaments with low making and wastage charges. We are totally dedicated to all our greatest assets - our customers and we are grateful to them for our success.
